<DESCRIPTION>
Enable rotation of start patterns and reversal of odd-indexed blocks by default to increase FirstFit pressure while maintaining omega. Additionally, always apply the micro-phase sprinkle when budget allows, instead of skipping it at six rounds. These tweaks restore the stronger behavior seen in prior best-performing variants (combined score 2.10) by diversifying overlap patterns across rounds and coupling colors late without inflating the clique number.
</DESCRIPTION>
