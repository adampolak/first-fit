enable_rotation_and_micro</NAME>

<DESCRIPTION>
Enable rotation of start patterns and activate all mixing options by default. We expand the start_patterns to include four rotation variants and set the defaults for rotate_starts, reverse_block_parity, interleave_blocks, and micro_phase to True. This increases cross-block interactions and sprinkles micro-phase gadgets, aiming to force FirstFit to use more colors without raising the clique number.
</DESCRIPTION>
