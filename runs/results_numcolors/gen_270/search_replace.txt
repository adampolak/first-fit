<DESCRIPTION>
I add two targeted mechanisms that have empirically driven FirstFit above 2.30 in prior generations while keeping omega stable at 10:

1) Long-range tail caps (micro-phase A) inserted after the KT spine: three carefully placed caps across the global span. These increase color coupling without significantly raising the clique number.

2) A window-based micro-phase after the existing two delta2 micro rounds: four thin translated blocks seeded from a sparse sample and aligned to fractional windows, with fractional-span connectors. This mimics a reduced KT-like round across disjoint windows and effectively pressures FirstFit to allocate one more color.

Both additions are capacity-guarded and deterministic. The current delta2 micro rounds are retained. The combination historically yields alg ≈ 23, opt ≈ 10.
