<DESCRIPTION>
Increase FirstFit pressure by enriching the micro-phase with additional long-range connectors and inter-window bridges, and by invoking two micro-rounds plus the parabolic phase by default. We change `phase2_iters` default to 2 and turn on `enable_parabolic`, and extend the connector list in `build_micro_delta_round` to include a “cross4” and two “bridge” intervals, filtering out degenerate ones. This should boost FF color usage without growing ω beyond 10.
</DESCRIPTION>
