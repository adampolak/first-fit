<NAME>eight_template_block_bank</NAME>
<DESCRIPTION>
Replace the 4-pattern start list with an 8-template bank and cycle through all 8 templates (A–H) each round and in the extra phase. This increases cross-scale diversity and disrupts repeating overlap patterns, boosting FirstFit pressure without increasing the clique number.
</DESCRIPTION>
