<DESCRIPTION>
Seed the initial interval set with multiple disjoint unit intervals to create richer early overlap coupling, and inject a small “micro-gadget” inside the median block each round. This adds dense local pressure on FirstFit without increasing the maximum clique, boosting the competitive ratio.
</DESCRIPTION>
