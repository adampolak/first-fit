- Enhance the micro-phase builder to support an alternate window set B: [(0.05,0.15), (0.28,0.38), (0.60,0.70), (0.82,0.92)] that targets different span regions than the primary micro-phase. This increases FirstFit pressure without inflating omega significantly.

- Slightly widen the thin seed for micro-rounds (up to 40 using len(T)//250) to leverage the late-stage structure without risking CAP, with strict trimming by budget.

These adjustments follow known heuristics: two-stage micro extensions, distinct window placement, and long-range couplers, all with strict capacity guards. The baseline spine remains unchanged. The overall count stays under 9800, while FF pressure is increased, aiming to improve the combined score beyond 2.30 in favorable cases.
