<DESCRIPTION>
Inject two extra long “cap” intervals at each expansion level within `_build` to couple the four towers more tightly. These caps span from 2→10 and 6→14 in units of the current delta, forcing FirstFit to open additional colors while only minimally affecting the clique size. This simple augmentation is inspired by the cap-augmented constructions and can raise the FF/ω ratio above 2.6.
