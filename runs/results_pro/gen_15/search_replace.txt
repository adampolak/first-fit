<DESCRIPTION>
Add a cyclic non-linear scaling schedule (SCALING_LIST) to modulate the expansion factor per recursion level. This replaces the fixed delta = hi - lo with delta = (hi - lo) * gamma, cycling gamma through SCALING_LIST, aiming to reduce clique growth while preserving FirstFit color growth.
</DESCRIPTION>
